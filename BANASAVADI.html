DESI MASALA - BANASWADI

Desi Masala, HRBR Layout, Banaswadi is situated in a conveniently accessible spot, close to the outer ring road, and on a wide 100-feet road.

Located in a primarily upscale residential area, this outlet attracts many diners. It has a spacious seatingcapacity for 80 diners and a rooftop party hall with a seating capacity for 250 diners.

Being the first outlet for Desi Masala, this branch generated a lot of goodwill among diners and propelled us to launch other branches.
