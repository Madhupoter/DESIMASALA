DESI MASALA - GANDHI NAGAR

Desi Masala is a vegetarian restaurant that specializes in traditional Indian cuisine. The restaurant is well-known for its unlimited offerings, providing customers with a never-ending supply of their favorite dishes..

Though Gandhi Nagar is already known for its iconic food outlets, Desi Masala Unlimited Buffet soon made a name for itself and became the talk of the town for its wide spread of dishes, taste, and quality.

So come and experience the true flavors of India at Desi Masala, where unlimited offerings and a passion for good food come together



At Desi Masala, the focus is on delivering delicious and healthy food in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're a regular customer or trying Indian cuisine for the first time, you're sure to be impressed by the variety and quality of the food on offer.
